NBA is a confusing bunch, especially if you re a new player. The Summoner s Rift map can be a daunting place for those starting out on their MOBA journeys, especially when you find yourself dying over and over. With over 130 champions in the game, the freedom of choice can feel overwhelming. It s going to take time to get to grips with how every champion plays, how they work together, and how to play against them. Luckily, the best way to learn all of this is to play the game and have fun!
